BungVision Label Studio v0.9.18 - Capture Width Tuning

- Reduced the Live Capture left panel width after v0.9.17 overcorrected.
- Left panel now has a practical min/max range instead of taking excessive screen space.
- Width and Height spinboxes are fixed to compact numeric widths.
- FPS and Preview scale controls are fixed to compact widths.
- Removed stretch behavior that made the Format row consume too much horizontal space.
- Kept previous fixes: JSON status indicators, safe Clear Boxes, Delete Saved JSON, custom classes, and standard YOLO box detection export.
