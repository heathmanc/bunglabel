BungVision Label Studio v0.9.17

Capture tab width/layout fix:
- Increased the left tab panel from the old recipe-sized rail to a wider resizable panel.
- Rebuilt Live Capture controls into a single-column form so fields do not require a cramped 300 px layout.
- Removed fixed minimum widths that forced clipping/overlap on the capture tab.
- Camera Options and Actions now stack vertically instead of being forced into multi-column grids.
- Enabled horizontal scrolling as a fallback for unusual Linux/Qt font scaling.

Carries forward v0.9.16/v0.9.14 fixes:
- JSON status indicators in captured image list.
- Clear Boxes does not overwrite/delete saved JSON.
- Delete Saved JSON is explicit.
- Custom classes restored.
- Standard YOLO box-detection export.
