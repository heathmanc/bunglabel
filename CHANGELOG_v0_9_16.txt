BungVision Label Studio v0.9.16
================================

Capture tab layout fix after v0.9.15 compact layout regression.

Changes:
- Rebuilt the Live Capture tab with a conservative scrollable stacked layout.
- Removed crowded nested form rows that could overlap on some Qt/Linux DPI/font combinations.
- Separated Camera / Stream, Camera Options, and Actions into clear groups.
- Kept Backend, Source, Width, Height, FPS, and Preview fields readable at normal and maximized window sizes.
- Kept the shorter Basler/Pylon hint, but only shows it when Basler/Pylon is selected.
- Preserved v0.9.14 behavior: JSON indicators, safer Clear Boxes, explicit Delete Saved JSON.
- Preserved v0.9.13 behavior: custom classes and standard YOLO box-detection export.
