BungVision Label Studio v0.9.27 - OBB UI polish

Built from v0.9.26 OBB battery + bung baseline.

Changes:
- Increased the left panel just enough to prevent clipped controls without returning to the oversized left rail.
- Disabled horizontal scrolling in the Live Capture tab so controls shrink/wrap instead of disappearing off the side.
- Moved the Exposure controls into their own compact group.
- Made Apply Exposure a full-width button under the manual exposure field instead of squeezing it into a crowded row.
- Made the Live Capture image action buttons full-width vertical buttons.
- Changed the Test Models action buttons from a four-button row to a 2x2 grid.
- Added high-contrast checkbox indicators for the dark theme so unchecked outlines are visible.
- Slightly reduced default button minimum-width pressure so rows are less likely to clip on Linux/Qt themes.

Preserved:
- OBB battery + OBB bung labeling workflow.
- Basler/Pylon open/capture path.
- Manual/auto exposure logic.
- Plain class workflow with no bung zones.
- Existing right-panel scroll/button sizing fixes.
